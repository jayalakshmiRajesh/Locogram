About Us


Locogram is a machine learning-powered discovery and shopping platform for women's apparel and accessories. Our vision is to bring all clothing stores that have embraced eCommerce under a single roof so that shoppers can easily find latest products and sales from their favorite stores as well as discover unique styles from thousands of one of a kind stores all over the US.

The Problem. Our goal is simple but the problem is challenging. Big brand stores are generally available on all major shopping platforms, but most of the small to medium size stores ("The Tail") are largely undiscoverable today. As a result, neither shoppers are able to easily discover products from these stores nor retailers are able to reach out to their potential customers. One of the key challenges that exists today is that managing presence on these platforms is still manual. Manually uploading products and constantly keeping them up to date require resources that most of the small retailers find difficult to manage.

Our Solution. We have developed a proprietary technology platform that uses advance machine learning techniques to automatically get all the products, categories and relevant product information from retailers' websites and create their dedicated storefronts on Locogram. Most importantly, these storefronts are automatically updated whenever information is updated on their websites. Unlike traditional web scrapers, which are brittle and unscalable, our platform is robust and highly-scalable: (a) it works for any clothing retailer, be it local, small, online or chain stores, and (b) it neither depends on their website design/layout nor depends on their backend technology platforms.

Our Unique Advantages. Locogram has several unique advantages for both shoppers and retailers. For retailers, it creates and manages their dedicated storefronts on our website and mobile apps without requiring any extra efforts from them to maintain yet another channel (completely automatic and hands-free). Furthermore, our discovery engine uses several intelligent techniques to easily get their storefronts or products discovered by their potential customers. For shoppers, Locogram is the largest virtual mall that contains all the stores that exist online. They get the most comprehensive and diverse collection of styles that they can get online. Further, our unique personalization engine allows every shopper to completely customize their shopping experience based on their taste, budget and preferences.
